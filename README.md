# All About Me 💛

A little iOS app I built during **Kode With Klossy**, my very first dive into coding.

### Where it started
Kode With Klossy is a free coding program for young women and gender‑expansive teens. It was my introduction to building software, and honestly the spark for everything I've made since. I learned Swift, built my first iOS apps, and got to do it surrounded by other girls who were just as excited to create something of their own.

### What it meant to me
Being a girl in STEM can feel like you're the only one in the room. KWK flipped that. For the first time I was in a space full of people who looked like me, all learning to build and cheering each other on. It taught me that I belong here, and that the things I make can actually matter.

### What I carried forward
That confidence turned into [Beyond the Sidelines](https://beyond-the-sidelines.com), a full recovery app I later designed, built, and shipped on my own. This little app is where it all began. 💛

Built with Swift and Xcode.
